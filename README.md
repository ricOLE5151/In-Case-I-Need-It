# In-Case-I-Need-It
You know those random ideas or thoughts you have that feel important in the moment, but you’re too lazy (or too overwhelmed) to actually organize? And then they’re just gone.

In Case I Need It lets you write things down, toss them somewhere, and revisit them later if you feel like it. or just leave them there forever. for overthinkers, idea hoarders, and people who don’t believe every thought needs a system.
